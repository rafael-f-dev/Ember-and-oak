Ember & Oak Coffee Roasters is a boutique coffee shop and roastery based in Portland, Oregon, specializing in handcrafted, small-batch coffee roasts. With a deep commitment to sustainability and quality, they source the finest beans directly from farmers and cooperatives around the world. Their in-house roasts offer a range of profiles, from bright and fruity to deep and chocolatey, ensuring every coffee lover finds their perfect brew.
